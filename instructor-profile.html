About Me

Hello! I'm Rajesh Kumar, a passionate web developer and educator with over 10 years of experience in the tech industry. I've worked with major companies like TCS and Infosys, and now I'm dedicated to sharing my knowledge with aspiring developers across India.

My teaching philosophy is centered around practical, hands-on learning. I believe the best way to learn programming is by building real projects. In my courses, you'll not only learn the theory but also apply your knowledge to create portfolio-worthy applications.

When I'm not coding or teaching, you can find me contributing to open-source projects, writing technical blogs, or exploring new web technologies that can benefit Indian developers.
